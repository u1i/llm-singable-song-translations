**Translation Notes & Justifications**

1. **"My love" → "Cintaku"**: Preserves emotional tone and meaning, while being a common expression in Bahasa Melayu.
2. **"There's only you in my life" → "Hanya kamu dalam hidupku"**: Matches syllable count and conveys the same exclusivity.
3. **"The only thing that's right" → "Satu-satunya yang benar"**: Preserves the rhyme scheme and emphasizes the correctness of the subject's presence.
4. **"My first love" → "Cinta pertamaku"**: Maintains the syllable count and emphasizes the primacy of the love.
5. **"You're every breath that I take" → "Kamu setiap nafas yang kuambil"**: Preserves the rhythm and emotional intensity.
6. **"And I / I want to share" → "Dan aku / Aku ingin kongsikan"**: Matches the syllable count and conveys the desire for sharing love.
7. **"No one else will do" → "Tidak ada yang lain"**: Preserves the exclusivity and simplicity of the original phrase.
8. **"And your eyes / They tell me how much you care" → "Dan matamu / Mereka ceritakan betapa kamu peduli"**: Maintains the rhythm and emphasizes the eyes as a window to the subject's care.
9. **"Oh yes, you will always be / My endless love" → "Oh ya, kamu sentiasa akan menjadi / Cinta abadiku"**: Preserves the emotional tone, rhyme scheme, and endless nature of the love.
10. **"Two hearts / Two hearts that beat as one" → "Dua Hati / Dua hati yang berdegup sebagai satu"**: Maintains the syllable count, rhythm, and emphasizes unity.
11. **"Our lives have just begun" → "Hidup kita baru bermula"**: Preserves the syllable count and conveys the beginning of a new life together.
12. **"Forever / I'll hold you close in my arms" → "Selamanya / Aku akan pelukmu erat dalam pelukanku"**: Maintains the emotional tone, rhythm, and emphasizes eternal closeness.
13. **"I'll be a fool for you, I'm sure / You know I don't mind" → "Aku akan jadi bodoh untukmu, aku yakin / Kamu tahu aku tidak keberatan"**: Preserves the syllable count, rhythm, and conveys the willingness to be foolish in love.
14. **"Cause you / You mean the world to me" → "Sebab kamu / Kamu bermakna dunia bagi aku"**: Maintains the emotional tone, rhythm, and emphasizes the subject's importance.
15. **"Oh, I know / I've found in you / My endless love" → "Oh, aku tahu / Aku temui dalam dirimu / Cinta abadiku"**: Preserves the emotional tone, rhyme scheme, and conveys the discovery of endless love.
