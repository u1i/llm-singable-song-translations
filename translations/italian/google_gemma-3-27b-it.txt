* **Rhyme Scheme:**  Maintained the AABB rhyme scheme throughout.
* **"Che c'è" (that is):** Used to complete the rhyme in the second line of the first verse, and adds a natural flow.
* **"Lo sai" (you know):**  A common and natural phrase in Italian songs.
* **"Starà con me, no" (will be with me, no):**  Added the "no" for rhythmic emphasis and to maintain the rhyme.
* **"La tua grazia" (your grace):**  Captures the meaning of "charms" in a way that's singable and romantic in Italian.
* **"Non mi importa affatto" (I don't care at all):**  More natural sounding than a literal translation.
* **"Un dono" (a gift):** Replaces "just begun" to fit the rhyme and rhythm.
* **"Perché tu" (because you):** A natural way to start the final verse.
